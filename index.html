First, a brief history of vehicles.

The first powered car

The first steam powered car was created by Nicolas-Joseph Cugnot in 1769 to haul artillery.

The first powered train

The first steam powered train was created by Richard Trevithick in 1804 to haul iron.

The first powered airplanes

The first airplane was created by the Wright brothers in 1903.
