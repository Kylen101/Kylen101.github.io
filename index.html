First, a brief history of vehicles.

The first powered car

The first steam powered car was created by Nicolas-Joseph Cugnot in 1769 to haul artillery.

The first powered train

The first steam powered train was created by Richard Trevithick in 1804 to haul iron.

The first powered airplanes

The first airplane was created by the Wright brothers in 1903.

Modern Vehicles

Old vehicles were cool, but new vehicles are (arguably, not trying to pick a fight here) cooler. The coolest vehicle is obviously a space ship (trying to pick a fight here), so here are some space shuttle pictures.


The Ford Mustang Mach-E is an electric car. It was released in 2019 and has a top speed of 124mph. It seats 5 people and has a large trunk and fronk (front trunk).


The Porsche 911 is a sport car that runs on gasoline. It was first created in 1963. The Porsche 911 has a top speed of 182mph and has seats for 2 people.

The Toyota Corolla is an everyday car that offers seats for 5 people. It was first created in 1966. The Toyota Corolla has a top speed of 117mph.

The Delta Airbus A220-100 is an incredibly common form of transportation for long distances. It seats roughly 170 people. The airplane has an average speed of 550mph.

Forklifts are a vehicle commonly used in the construction field. It is used to lift heavy objects from one place to another.

Trains are used to transport large objects far distances. The average speed of the commerical train is 120mph.

Princess Cruise Ships offer a form of vacation for many people. Princess Cruise Ships hold roughly 3,000-4,000 people.
